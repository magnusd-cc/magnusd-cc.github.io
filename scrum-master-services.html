Scrum Master services

How may I help you?

Starting autumn 2019, I'm offering my services on a short-term freelance basis.

Using my experiences and insights from more than than a decade working with agile teams using Scrum and Kanban, some of the things I'm able to help you with are:

  • Facilitation of workshops and meetings, on-site or remotely. Some examples of what I can help you with are team initiations / kick-offs, sprint or project retrospectives, user story mapping, impact mapping, etc etc.
  • Mentoring and coaching of Scrum Masters, either helping those new to the role or those looking to grow.
  • Short term Scrum Master or Agile Coaching assignments, on-site or remotely. I can help a team get going with Scrum or, for example, provide temporary cover when the Scrum Master is away or during a short project.
