Hello, I am Magnus Dahlgren

I’m a freelance Scrum Master and Agile Coach based in London, United Kingdom.

I started out as a software developer but have been working as a Scrum Master and Agile Coach in some shape or form for the past 12 years. I love helping teams deliver awesome products, supporting them and those around them in finding approaches and ways of working that work for them.

How I can help you

Through my company, Magnus Dahlgren Enterprises, I’m available on a short-term freelance basis to help you make the most of agile.

Agile coaching of teams and individuals

Agile processes is not something that’s “owned” by a coach or a Scrum Master, and throughout my agile career, my focus has been on helping teams find their own solutions. Using experiences and insights from more than than a decade working with agile teams using Scrum and Kanban, I can provide the following services:

  • Coaching of teams and individuals, helping them find solutions to their own problems and moving beyond “doing agile”, as a list of practices, to being agile, with the shift in mindset it requires.
  • Mentoring, for example guiding new Scrum Masters as they learn their new role
  • Facilitation of various events, as described below
  • Teaching, introducing various agile concepts and techniques

Contact me on me@magnusd.cc to discuss how I can help you.

One-off facilitation of workshops, Scrum events and other sessions

There are many reasons why it can be a good idea to bring in an external facilitator for a meeting or workshop. Not only is an experienced and neutral facilitator a way to increase the chances of achieving the desired outcomes. It also frees up everyone else up take active part in the session and can be an opportunity to observe how to run the session, so that it can be run by someone internal next time.

Over the years, I have run countless workshops and meetings and I can help you facilitate pretty much any session, including:

  • Team initiations / kick-offs
  • Sprint or project retrospectives
  • Brainstorming
  • Story mapping
  • Troubleshooting
  • etc etc

Contact me on me@magnusd.cc to find out more.
