Let's do this! Again.

I used to blog quite a bit about Scrum and Agile a couple of years ago. My old blog is still there, in all its glory. But then I stopped for whatever reason. Not through an active decision, the letting the gap inbetween posts grow. A week became a month, became a year, became two.

I've started to miss it though. I enjoy writing. And as I enjoy reading about others' experiences of agile, it only seems right to share mine. So I'm starting again.

This time around, my intention is to try and focus as much as I can on practices and actionable takeaways. Agile may be a mindset but at the end of the day, it's what we actually do, based on whatever that mindset is, that allows us to become agile.

Welcome to Magnus' Scrum Master blog, take 2!
